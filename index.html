About

Born and raised on the main streets of Mississauga, DJ Masalah, started his musical journey over 10 years. This was the moment that he knew he wanted to be part of 'The Scene'

Influenced by various genres, DJ Masalah developed his unique style by consistently experimenting with acoustic landscape of hooks and innovative mixing methods. Despite the difficulties of 2020, DJ Masalah is a fighter and has since been locked in the studio honing his craft in preparation for the perfect moment.

Suffice to say, the Bollywood mixing scene should be expecting big things from DJ Masalah in 2021.
