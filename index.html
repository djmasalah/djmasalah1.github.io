About

DJ Masalah started spinning and quickly earned a name for himself to become a Bollywood DJ in the GTA. He became interested in music at a young age and influenced by genres including Bollywood, Pop, and Hip-Hop. DJ Masalah developed his unique style by experimenting with an acoustic landscape of hooks and innovative mixing methods.
